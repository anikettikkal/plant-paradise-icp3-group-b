Steps to start your plants off right


Inoculate

The use of soil inoculants has promise
for use in agricultural systems for improving
nutrient status, reducing plant diseases
and pests, and improving yields.



Sow the Seeds

Sowing is the process of planting seeds
into the soil. During this proper precaution
should be taken, soil should be clean,healthy
and free from disease.

Disinfecting pots is a simple step to
prepare for a successful spring garden
ahead of planting.
